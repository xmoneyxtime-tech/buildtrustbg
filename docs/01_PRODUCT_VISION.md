# BTOS Product Vision

## Purpose
Describe the long-term product strategy for BuildTrustBG as a trusted SaaS ecosystem for construction companies.

## Current Status
Active, aligned with current stack: Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, Prisma, Neon, Auth.js v5, TailwindCSS.

## Strategic Vision
BuildTrustBG evolves from trusted discovery into the operating system for construction businesses.

The platform must help companies:
- build measurable trust
- improve visibility and conversion
- manage reputation and customer communication
- scale operations with AI assistance

The platform must help customers:
- evaluate companies safely
- compare credibility signals quickly
- contact the right company with confidence

## Product Outcomes
- Trust: transparent, verifiable company data and behavior
- SEO: structured, indexable company and service surfaces
- Conversion: high-intent profile pages and clear CTAs
- Retention: dashboards that guide companies toward better outcomes
- Scalability: modular domains and future internationalization

## Strategic Modules
- Identity and access
- Company profile and verification
- Reputation and reviews
- Portfolio/projects/gallery
- Leads, inquiries, and messaging
- Premium and billing
- Analytics and recommendations
- AI insights and optimization

## Future Roadmap Direction
- v1.x: robust trust profile and marketplace foundation
- v1.5: business assistant dashboard and actionable guidance
- v2.x: end-to-end workflow management for companies

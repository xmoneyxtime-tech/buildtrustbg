# BTOS Roadmap

## Purpose
Define phased delivery strategy by version with business and technical outcomes.

## Current Status
Active planning artifact, aligned with current architecture and product priorities.

## Versioned Plan

### v1.0 - Trusted Marketplace Foundation
- Production-grade identity and role protection
- Company application workflow and moderation
- Public company profile baseline
- SEO-ready pages and structured content foundation
- Initial trust signals and profile completeness indicators

### v1.5 - Company Growth Assistant
- Intelligent company dashboard KPIs
- Actionable recommendations for trust and visibility
- Review management and response workflows
- Expanded portfolio/projects/gallery management

### v2.0 - Business Operations Core
- Leads/inquiries pipeline
- Messaging and customer communication center
- Document and certificate management
- Subscription and premium tooling

### v2.5 - Commercial Scale
- Payments and billing reliability hardening
- Advanced analytics and conversion insights
- AI recommendations for profile and reputation optimization

### v3.0 - International Expansion Readiness
- Multi-language and region-aware content strategy
- Localization-ready marketplace taxonomy
- Partner and enterprise integrations

## Future Roadmap Maintenance
- Update after each release cycle
- Map features to trust/SEO/conversion impact

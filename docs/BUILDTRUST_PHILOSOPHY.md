# BuildTrust Philosophy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active  
**Project:** BuildTrustBG

---

# Mission

BuildTrustBG exists to increase trust between customers and construction businesses.

Every product decision, architectural decision and business decision must support this mission.

The platform is designed to reward transparency, professionalism and long-term reputation.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Trust First

Every feature must increase trust.

If a feature does not improve trust between customers and businesses, it is not a priority.

---

## 2. Transparency

Information should be complete, understandable and verifiable.

Hidden information reduces trust.

---

## 3. Quality over Quantity

We prefer fewer, high-quality companies over thousands of unverified listings.

---

## 4. Reputation Must Be Earned

Trust cannot be purchased.

Premium subscriptions never influence reputation or ratings.

The BuildTrust Score™ is always calculated automatically.

---

## 5. AI Assists — Humans Decide

Artificial Intelligence supports decisions.

Artificial Intelligence never replaces administrative responsibility.

Critical decisions remain under human control.

---

## 6. Security by Design

Security is considered from the beginning of development.

Authentication, authorization and data protection are fundamental requirements.

---

## 7. Long-Term Architecture

Every architectural decision must make the platform easier to extend, never easier only to implement.

Short-term shortcuts that compromise scalability are avoided.

---

## 8. User Experience

Complex systems should feel simple.

Users should accomplish their goals with minimal effort.

---

## 9. Data Integrity

Data must be reliable, consistent and traceable.

Business-critical information is never silently modified or removed.

---

## 10. Continuous Improvement

BuildTrustBG is a continuously evolving platform.

Every release should improve quality, performance or customer trust.

# BTOS Project Manifesto

## Purpose
Define the foundational mission and non-negotiable principles for BuildTrust Operating System (BTOS), the product core behind BuildTrustBG.

## Current Status
Active and mandatory for all product, engineering, and operational decisions.

## Manifesto
BuildTrustBG is not a directory. It is a trust ecosystem and SaaS platform for the construction industry.

Every implementation must answer:
Does this improve trust, visibility, and business outcomes for construction companies while helping customers choose safely and confidently?

Core commitments:
- Trust first
- Real companies and truthful data only
- Reputation is earned, not purchased
- Security and integrity by design
- Scalable architecture over short-term shortcuts
- SEO-first discoverability and strong UX
- Measurable business impact per feature

## Platform Pillars
- Trust Infrastructure: verification, transparent profiles, quality signals
- Business Enablement: leads, messaging, reputation, growth tools
- Marketplace Discovery: SEO-ready content, filtering, city/category structure
- Intelligence Layer: AI-assisted insights and recommendations

## Roadmap Intent
- v1.x: trusted company profiles, moderation workflows, reputation baseline
- v2.x: complete operating workflows for company growth and customer lifecycle
- v3.x: AI-driven optimization and international scalability

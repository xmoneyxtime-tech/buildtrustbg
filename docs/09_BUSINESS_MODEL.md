# BTOS Business Model

## Purpose
Define value creation, monetization strategy, and guardrails that preserve platform trust.

## Current Status
Active strategy baseline; monetization expansion planned in phased releases.

## Value Proposition
- For companies: more trust, better visibility, more qualified inquiries
- For customers: safer selection and higher confidence in contractor choice

## Revenue Streams
- Premium subscriptions
- Featured visibility placements
- Verification-related service packages
- Future paid lead products and business tools

## Non-Negotiable Rule
Trust cannot be purchased. Paid features may improve visibility and tooling, but must not manipulate trust scoring integrity.

## Future Roadmap
- v1.5: premium profile tooling improvements
- v2.0: billing and subscription lifecycle hardening
- v2.5: growth analytics and ROI surfaces for companies

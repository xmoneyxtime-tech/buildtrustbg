# BTOS AI Strategy

## Purpose
Define how AI improves trust, performance, and business outcomes without replacing human accountability.

## Current Status
Early implementation stage with foundational AI review capabilities and planned expansion.

## AI Principles
- AI assists; humans decide critical outcomes
- Transparency over black-box decisions
- Never fabricate companies, reviews, or business claims
- Keep trust scoring interpretable and auditable

## AI Product Focus
- Review sentiment and insight extraction
- Company improvement recommendations
- Profile optimization guidance
- SEO and visibility suggestions for companies

## Safety and Governance
- Validate and constrain AI outputs
- Log decision context for admin-facing actions
- Separate recommendation from enforcement

## Future Roadmap
- v1.5: recommendation engine in company dashboard
- v2.0: AI-assisted lead qualification and response support
- v2.5: predictive trust/visibility analytics
